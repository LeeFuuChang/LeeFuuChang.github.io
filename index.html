About Me 關於我

My name is Lee FuuChang, and I am a Senior-High student, I specifically love programming AI related stuffs. I love spending time on debuging, optimizing my program, doing some Data-Analyzing, which are also the basics of Programming and doing Machine Learning. I also enjoying discussing with other programers, as the saying goes "If you live with a lame person you will learn to limp", hope I can keep learning from them and improve myself.
